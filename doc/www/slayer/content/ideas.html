SLAYER: ideas (ideas)

As a framework, SLAYER is very general and flexible, and it is important that its extensions don't loose that flexibility.

One of the ideas is to extend the <text-area> widget used in the "slayer.scm" demo to give it more editing capabilities (especially when it comes to working with the Scheme sources). It could eventually be regarded as a multimedia-enabled emacs that would be more friendly to newcomers.

Another idea would be to port SLAYER to various environments, like web browsers (via some of the Scheme-to-JavaScript compilers), or to integrate it with some of the implementations like Bigloo, Chicken or Gambit that generate stand-alone executables.
